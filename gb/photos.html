Some quotes from Gordon's family...

"We so appreciate you having the interest and taking the time to put all this information together. I assure you that Dad would have been amazed by all of it. It will be a wonderful memorial of him for our children and grandchildren. Tim and I have 14 grandsons and 1 granddaughter, so in the coming years, this will be a well-accessed source of information. Dad’s oldest great grandchild is in Gr. 12 and for him to be able to hear Dad’s voice telling his own story, makes this part of our history, very meaningful to him."
- Bonnie Perrett (daughter)


"So awesome that we can hear Grandpa's stories. You forget how people sound and it was a nice reminder of how articulate and methodical he was when he spoke. He had a real knack for telling them in a way that drew you in, even if it was just about milk or oranges."
- Chena Perrett (grand-daughter)


"Your tribute to Dad is wonderful and one that our family will treasure for years to come. I was so pleased that you were able to keep his voice in the story. This will be forwarded on to the rest of our family and discussed many times, once again many thanks."
- Terry Baker (son)
